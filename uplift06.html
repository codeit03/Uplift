UpliftU is an inspiring startup founded by IIT Bombay students belonging to diverse Branches. They bring their experience and are dedicated to providing mentorship for students who are uncertain about which college or branch to choose. By offering personalized guidance and insightful advice, UpliftU aims to empower students to make informed decisions about their academic paths, ensuring they embark on a journey that aligns with their passions and goals.
